Everything you need to know

Questions Worth
Answering Honestly.

How long does it take to build my website?

Most projects are completed within 3 to 5 days from the start of discovery. Timelines are determined by the complexity of the project and the responsiveness of both parties during the feedback process. Rushed work produces average results, this practice does not do average.

Do you only work with businesses in India?

Currently, the primary focus is on service businesses operating within India. All communication is conducted remotely via WhatsApp and video call, making location entirely irrelevant to the quality of the collaboration.

Why is there no pricing on the website?

Because your business is not a commodity and neither is this work. Every project is scoped individually based on what your specific situation demands. A discovery conversation costs nothing and will tell you everything you need to make an informed decision.

Can you redesign my existing website rather than build from scratch?

Yes. A full audit of your existing site is conducted first to diagnose what is working, what is not, and what is actively costing you clients. The scope is then determined by what the diagnosis reveals, not by an arbitrary package.

I have never hired a web designer before. Where do I begin?

You begin with a single WhatsApp message. There is no complex onboarding process, no lengthy forms. A brief conversation will establish whether this is the right fit: for both of us. If it is, we move forward. If it is not, I will tell you honestly.

Why should I not just use a website builder like Wix or Squarespace?

Website builders are designed for the average business producing an average result. If your ambition is to be indistinguishable from your competitors, a builder will serve you adequately. If your ambition is to be the obvious choice in your market, the architecture of a purpose-built, conversion-engineered website is foundational.
